Rulla Hatt Seltzer

About Us

One faithful day, on a vacation in Mexico, four friends were sitting in a bar having some drinks. After a while, they decided to order a round of seltzers.
Upon tasting the seltzers, they all looked at eachother with disappointed faces.
"This was not good".
"It doesn't taste anything".
"I should have ordered something else".
And then, jokingly, one of them uttered "If we made a seltzer it would be a lot better than this". And boy, were they right.
That moment became the starting point for Rulla Hatt.

Did you know?

"Rulla hatt" is Swedish for "Roll the hat", which is an old-timey expression for going out to party. This explains all the hats you see on our products and website.
